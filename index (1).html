About

An engineer who likes to build, test, and try again.

I'm Chen Gao — a student with a strong interest in math, physics, and computer science, and a track record of turning that into things that actually work: robots that hold up under competition pressure, code that runs, and ideas that survive contact with reality.

On the robotics side, I've competed in VEX IQ for three seasons and qualified for the VEX IQ World Championship every single time — across the Full Volume, Rapid Relay, and Mix & Match games. Along the way my teams have earned Excellence, Teamwork Champion, Build, and Robot Skills awards at the regional and state level.

Outside of robotics, I'm building a foundation in math, physics, and computer science through coursework and STEM competitions — and I'm currently preparing to step up from VEX IQ into both VEX V5 and FTC, bringing the same focus on iteration, problem-solving, and team strategy to bigger fields and bigger robots.
